Welcome to Progressive Option Support Services (POSS)

Progressive Option Support Services is a multi-specialty agency dedicated to providing a wide variety of therapeutic and behavioral services to youth and families experiencing developmental, emotional, and behavioral challenges. We strive to provide the best services with the highest regard to quality, safety, and satisfaction.

Our Mission

At Progressive Option Support Services, our mission is to strengthen families by utilizing highly effective, evidence-based, therapeutic supports to promote the progress and well-being of children and adolescents through prevention, intervention, education, and advocacy.

We pride ourselves on being a community resource that works closely with our community partners to provide direct care for children and families that reflect our agencyâ€™s high standards for excellence. We believe that successful collaboration and teamwork with parents/caregivers are not only keys to success, but help to support an overall positive, meaningful, and sustainable outcome. POSS celebrates and honors uniqueness and diversity of all individuals.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to transform the behavioral healthcare system by promoting health and wellness, focusing on sustainable change, and ensuring access to supports and services for children and their families.
